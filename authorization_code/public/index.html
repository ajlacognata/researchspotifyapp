AP Research Music-Politics Correlation

Consent

Purpose of this study:

The purpose of this study is to collect data in order to compare one's political views with their preferred music taste in order to discover if there are any correlations between politics and their preffered music taste.

Anonymity:

All responses will remain anonymous and cannot be traced back to the participant. Responses can only be seen by the researcher and will only be used within this research paper. The Spotify login button will take you through Spotify's secure log in site that the researcher will have no access to. Spotify will then return only the participant's top 50 tracks and artists of all time.

Data Collected:

This study will collect participant's top 50 Spotify tracks and artists, political orientation, personality type, and demographics such as high school grade level, gender, ethnicity, and area of the country (demographic questions will have an other or prefer not to say answer)

Participation Requirements:

To participate in this survey, you must be a member of Generation Z (1996-2010) in the United States, have a Spotify Account, and agree to all conditions within the consent form.

Instructions

Participants will log into their Spotify account using the button below. This will give the researcher their top 50 tracks and artists of all time but will not give the researcher any other data pertaining to the participant's Spotify account. All login handling is done through Spotify's servers per their security protocols. The user will then be given a number to write down/remember to input in the first question on the survey portion of the research. The user will also then be given a link to a Microsoft Forms survey. This survey will be used to assess the participant's political orientation, personality type, birth year range, grade level if applicable, and collect any other demographics such as sex and race/ethnicity.
